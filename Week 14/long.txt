Formula 1 (F1) is one of the most famous motorsport championships in the world.
It features single-seater racing cars that compete on circuits across various countries, showcasing the highest levels of speed and technology.
Victory depends on driver skill, team strategy, engineering, tire management, and many other factors.

Each year, F1 consists of multiple Grand Prix races, with qualifying and main events at every venue.
Points are awarded for both the Drivers’ Championship and the Constructors’ Championship, bringing prestige to individuals and teams alike.

Recently, F1 has embraced eco-friendly technologies, hybrid engines, and advanced aerodynamics, greatly influencing the automotive industry.
